Some Best North Indian Non-veg Foods

1. Whether or not you are a rice lover, the aroma emanated from a plate of biryani is bound to stir the excitement of your taste buds.Basmati rice and the tenderly cooked meat (or vegetables), the Lucknawi or the Awadhi Biryani is a feast in itself. The royal dish is best served hot with a simple raita (curd).

2. What better than the spicy Chicken Butter Masala to devour along with Naan or Rumali Roti. The distinct flavor of fenugreek leaves or kasuri methi and the chicken cooked in butter till tender is the Holy Grail for any non-vegetarian.The gravy is prepared with fresh cream and the infusion of spices, especially the Kashmiri red chili powder which imparts a tantalizing color to the dish.

3. The tandoor, which is a cylindrical clay oven that is used for baking or cooking, first originated in the state of Punjab.One among the tandoori delicacy is the tandoori chicken which is a roasted chicken that is marinated in curd and basted with a mélange of spices including Cayenne pepper and red chili powder.

4. A fiery meat curry from Rajasthan, this one is loaded with red chillies. Mutton is cooked in mustard oil and flavoured with the local kachri spice. This one is surely not for the faint-hearted! Burst of spices and goodness of meat.

5. Punjabi Mutton Curry is a quintessential Punjabi recipe that is quite popular among the Indian foodies who dig spicy and delicious dishes. This curry recipe is loaded with wholesome spices that are cooked in desi ghee.

6. Punjabi Egg curry is a delicious spicy curry. Eggs are first boiled, then peeled and then added to the spicy gravy and slowly simmered into it, so that Eggs get the flavor from the gravy. The gravy is made using onions, garlic, ginger, chilies, tomatoes, and a combination of Indian spices.

7. Egg Slice Masala is a tasty twist to our very own egg curry. This delectable dish is cooked in tangy-spicy gravy which gives it a unique flavour and fragrance.

8. Sarsoon machhi or sarson machhi is a typical Bengali fish dish with mustard flavours. Fresh fish such as hilsa tastes best in this recipe.

9. This tawa fish fry recipe is modelled after Amritsari fried fish – a spicy, masala coated fish fry that is a popular street food in North India, especially in Punjab..
