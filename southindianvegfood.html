Some Best South Indian Veg Foods

1. Masala dosa is the most popular South Indian dish loved by people across world. It is found all over India and in few overseas countries.Dosa batter is made by soaking rice, urud dal and lentils overnight and then grinding the same to form a batter.

2. Rava idly is mostly prepared for breakfast using semolina as main ingredient. It is one of the best variant of idly found all over South India.

3. Vada is fried dish mostly consumed during breakfast. The dish is made using paste of legumes like (arhar) piegon pea, (chana) chickpea, vigna mungo (urad) mung, etc.The term ‘medu vada’ means ‘soft’ in Kannada. These vada are usually soft inside and crispy outside, made in the shape of a doughnut using black lentils batter.

4. Uttappam is a very special dish of Tamil Nadu, which is found all over India. It is the ticker version of dosa topped with onions, green chillies, capsicum and tomatoes. Ready batter and instant mixes are also available in market.

5. Upma is rice porridge that is mixed with chili and onions and generally served hot. It is another popular breakfast item in South India.

6. Veg Biryani in pressure cooker is a one pot dish, but surprisingly every way as tasty and flavorful as the one cooked in traditional way.Veg Biryani is a mixed rice dish popular in entire Indian subcontinent and among the Indian, Pakistani and Bangladeshi people around the globe. Vegetable Biryani is made with rice, avariety of spices, lentils and vegetables.

7. Sambhar is a popular South Indian dish which is served as an accompaniment with Dosa, Idli and Vada. It is a healthy recipe as it is loaded with vegetables. It is one of one most loved South Indian recipe
